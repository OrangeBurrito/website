Following my last project working on the frontend of Arajet's [Arabonito](https://arabonito.arajet.com) site, I was able to make good use of the same design principles and visual language when taking on the task of building the Referrals program.

## Design
During the initial design process, I began building the Figma mock-ups, with highly interactive prototypes (on both mobile and desktop) to meet the Referrals app's comparatively more complex feature requirements.

## Development
From my previous work on Arabonito, I was able to reapply my preexisting component library for the new Referrals project, with modifications to the library, from general refactoring to the visual, according to Arajet's new style guide.
